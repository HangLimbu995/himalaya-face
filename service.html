FAQ

Are our designs safe with you?

Your designs are 100% safe with us. As part of our ‘code of conduct’ your designs will not be shared with anyone that isn’t involved with the manufacturing of your product.

What kind of testing do you do?

As part of our manufacturing services, each product gets a risk analysis and a testing menu is made based on product and fabric characteristics. Common tests we do are home laundering, colour fastness, shrinkage, anti-pilling, and seam strength. Other tests can be done on request at additional costs.

How long does it take to source a fabric?

We dedicate a lot of time and resources to the sourcing of materials. Most well known materials can be sourced in a matter of days. Some more new & exclusive fabrics can take bit longer because we need to check with more than one fabric supplier in different parts of the country. In rare cases the fabric you want might not be available and it will need to be custom developed for you. That means the fabric mill will need to make a trail yardage for you. This is usually only in rare cases where nothing similar at all is available.

Do you provide salesman samples?

While we don’t often make salesman samples, we understand that some brands require them as part of their selling format. For brands which have been established on the market for a long time and can easily meet all the minimums, this is a service we would like to try and offer. For new brands which aren’t established yet we can only offer salesman samples if it’s combined with a confirmed purchase order that meets the MOQ.

How can I get a quote?

After we have all the details we need from you such as your tech pack and quality reference materials we will need about 4-5 days to give you an idea of the costing. The costing will be confirmed after sampling which takes another 1-2 weeks after quotations.

Which brands do you work with?

Currently, our own brand is thriving in the market, and as a result, we are not collaborating with any other brands at this time.
