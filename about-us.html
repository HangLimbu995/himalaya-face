From the motherland of himalaya's

himalayan face Garments, based in kathmandu, nepal, proudly upholds a rich tradition in textiles that traces back to the early era of trekking. For decades, the region has been a thriving hub for trekking trousers manufacturers, establishing itself as a leading destination for high-quality trekking apparel production and renowned brands.

Our office and clothing factories are strategically located at the center of places known for clothing shops. This advantageous location makes it easier for us to stay connected with the latest trends and understand exactly what our customers want.

As a wholesale supplier, we cater to retailers and businesses worldwide. Our extensive range of trekking trousers, short trousers, and jackets is designed to meet the diverse needs of outdoor enthusiasts. Whether it's tackling rugged terrains or exploring urban landscapes, our garments provide optimum comfort, protection, and style.

Being a factory ourselves, we have full control over the manufacturing process, allowing us to maintain strict quality control and deliver exceptional products at competitive prices. We use premium-grade fabrics that are carefully selected for their durability, breathability, and moisture-wicking properties, ensuring our garments perform exceptionally even in the most demanding conditions.

We take immense pride in our products and strive to exceed the expectations of our customers. Whether you're a retailer looking to stock your shelves with high-quality outdoor apparel or an adventure enthusiast seeking the perfect gear, trust Himalayan Face Garments to deliver unparalleled quality and service.
